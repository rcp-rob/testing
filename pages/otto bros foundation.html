Culture

POWER’s Culture is like that of a close family who care for each other as brothers and sisters, guided by 'The POWER Way'.

"Our culture is evidence to our Family's belief in our Values. We cheerfully give more than what is asked of us, making sure that our business, which we treat as our Home, is always respectfully clean for our honoured guest - the customer.

Together with our brothers and sisters, we make sure that our guests’ visit to our House is a memorable one, with us having added value to their lives and leaving them feeling good about themselves.

Our family grows through the birth of new stores, which we care for as we would for a new born baby, growing into further representations of the POWER culture."
